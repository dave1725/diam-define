User-Flow
1) Choose an arena if its free from DB
2) Deposit 2 USDC to the arena to cover the premium
3) enter loan amount in USDC
4) request flashloan for amount
5) withdraw arbitrage amount to account
6) set arena free in DB
7) Flash loans completed!

set-up
1) deploy DEX.sol
2) add liquidity of 5k USDC and 5k DAI
3) deploy flash loan arbitrage contract with dex aaddres - Arena1
4) approve 100k DAI and 100k USDC
5) ready to request loan!
